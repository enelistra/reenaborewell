We are the leaders in bore well industry, specialises in professional bore well drilling throughout Kerala

Our company was established in 1990, and since its inception has undertaken various contracts for various types of bore well, calix, tube well, water exploration, dewatering boreholes, earth pits etc. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as their products and services, have helped this establishment garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow by the day.

Reena Borewells has a leading role in drilling industry with the dedicated, advanced and innovative staff and geologists who offer excellent drilling services which ensures accurate utilization of resources and give correct advises on availability, location, diameter, depth, purity and costs in a geological perspective. We provide a total drilling solution for our clients with all wide range of modern machineries.

Reena Borewells is an inhouse specialist in super fast high power rigs. We also undertake installations; repairs of all types of Borewells, Calix, Rotary, Earth Point, Cleaning, Yield testing etc.

Drilling is done in all soils including clay & rocks. Another peculiarity is we can offer Borewells where the digging point is 600 meters away from the road. Reena borewells is a registered drilling contractor with the Ground water Department Kerala proving integrated solutions from well design to completion in various surfaces and underground conditions.

Reena borewells owns a large fleet with a rated capacity up to 2000 Ft. Our services has been associated with the various types and diameter of borewell / tubewell / handbore drilling and rig supply in industry of South India for over 30 years. We supply all our rigs and modern drilling and survey equipments to all other contractors who work in Kerala. For this all our percussion tools, backed with support vehicles and ancillary equipments, staffs and machinery are working in a 24 x 7 manner even in shifts

Services

We are serving rural & city clients of Kerala Since 1990 and have expertise in drilling 4½",5", 6½",7" & 8½" bore-wells with super fast hydraulic rigs and experienced-qualified geologists. We have also Groundwater Surveyors, who assist the customer in deciding the borewell point; installation of submersible, jet & hand pumps.Specialist in & cleaning Damaged Borewells, Earth pits, all types of borewell drilling for Domestic & Industrial purpose, Site chosen by scientific method, Compressor hiring and borewell servicing etc. We operate an extensive range of drills, from modern table and top drive multi-purpose rigs to percussion cable tools, backed with support vehicles and ancillary equipment to cover most phases of bore hole drilling.
